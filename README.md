# 🏦 Code Alpha Fintech: Strategic Audit & Risk Mitigation Dashboard 🚀

## 📌 Executive Summary
This project is a high-stakes strategic audit of a *₦494M* Fintech transaction portfolio. By integrating *PostgreSQL* for deep-data interrogation and *Power BI* for diagnostic visualization, I identified critical operational leakages and untapped revenue growth opportunities. This analysis focuses on moving from "What happened?" to "What should the business do next?"

---

## 🚀 Top 4 Strategic Insights & Business Decisions

### 1. The "Lagos Risk" Diagnostic (Credit Strategy)
* *The Discovery:* A deep-dive into *Tier 1 (unverified)* users revealed that *Lagos* accounts for the highest *Delinquency Rate (26.79%)* while simultaneously generating the lowest revenue for that segment.
* *The Decision:* Recommended an immediate *freeze on Tier 1 lending in Lagos*. New credit facilities in this region should be restricted to Tier 2/3 verified users to protect company capital from high-default unverified accounts.

### 2. "Whale" Retention Strategy (Revenue Stability)
* *The Discovery:* Using SQL Window Functions and CTEs, I identified that a specialized group of *11.9% of users (The Whales)* drive the vast majority of the company's *₦494M* total volume.
* *The Decision:* Proposed the launch of an *"Idan VIP" Loyalty Program*. By offering tiered benefits to this top 11.9%, we secure the core revenue base against competitor poaching.

### 3. The ₦2 Million Revenue Recovery (Growth Hack)
* *The Discovery:* Audit identified *1,500 "Silent" Users* who successfully completed KYC registration but have zero transaction volume.
* *The Decision:* Calculated a conservative *₦2.00M potential recovery* opportunity. Recommended a targeted re-engagement campaign to convert these dormant leads into active revenue generators.

### 4. Regulatory & Compliance "Firewall"
* *The Discovery:* Tier 1 users were found to have an *Average Ticket Size of ₦49.8K*, which is statistically higher than many verified users.
* *The Decision:* Recommended a *₦20,000 transaction cap* for Tier 1 users to force organic migration to Tier 3 (Full Verification) and mitigate Anti-Money Laundering (AML) risks.
